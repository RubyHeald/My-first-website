Chasing the green ticks

Are you chasing the green ticks?

Starting the Multiverse Bootcamp is a daily crash course in learning! It’s easy to become fixated on completing the modules for the sense of accomplishment given from the green tick. It means you can move onto the endless amount of pending work that’s on your mind for the day. Questioning how are you going to be able to complete it all? Working with people in the cohort to collaboratively chase the tick. I have learnt that chasing the tick does not bring clarity on the module.

Friday comes and the end of week project is looming…

I had to stop, think, and reevaluate my learning style.

I began asking myself the following questions:

  • Does the green tick confirm I understood the assignment?
  • Do I understand the content?
  • Have I visited the external links for further learning?
